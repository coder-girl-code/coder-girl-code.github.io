Piano

The piano is a stringed keyboard instrument in which the strings are struck by wooden hammers that are coated with a softer material (modern hammers are covered with dense wool felt; some early pianos used leather).

Guitar

Flute

The flute is a classical music instrument in the woodwind group. Flutes are aerophones, meaning they make sound by vibrating a column of air. A flute is an instrument makes sound with the flow of air across an opening.

Violin

The Violin typically has four strings (some can have five), and is most commonly played by drawing a bow across its strings. It can also be played by plucking the strings with the fingers (pizzicato)

Double Bass

The double bass is played with a bow (arco), or by plucking the strings (pizzicato), or via a variety of extended techniques. In orchestral repertoire and tango music, both arco and pizzicato are employed.
